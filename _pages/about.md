## About Me

Dr. Yifu Ou is an EU-funded Marie Curie Fellow at Heriot-Watt University (United Kingdom) and Associate Editor of *All Earth*.

His research lies at the intersection of Geographic Data Science, Urban Geography, Regional Planning, Transport Studies, and Environmental Sustainability. His work investigates how infrastructure investment, environmental change, and urban development influence human mobility, accessibility, spatial inequalities, and environmental outcomes across cities and regions.

His combines GIS, spatial analysis, econometrics, machine learning, and input-output analysis to generate policy-relevant evidence for sustainable urban and regional planning. His work has been published in leading international journals including *Land Use Policy*, *Sustainable Cities and Society*, *Transportation Research Part D*, *Journal of Environmental Management*, and *Humanities & Social Sciences Communications*.

## 关于我

欧逸夫博士现任英国赫瑞瓦特大学（Heriot-Watt University）玛丽·居里学者（Marie Curie Fellow），并担任 SCI 期刊 *All Earth* 副主编。

研究领域涵盖地理数据科学、城市与区域规划、交通与出行、环境可持续发展、循环经济与气候变化。主要利用 GIS、空间分析、计量经济学、机器学习和投入产出分析等方法，研究基础设施建设、环境变化和城市发展对居民出行、空间公平、碳排放及环境质量的影响。

相关成果发表于 *Land Use Policy*、*Sustainable Cities and Society*、*Transportation Research Part D*、*Journal of Environmental Management* 等国际期刊。
