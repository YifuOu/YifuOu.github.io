# Research Themes

## Geographic Data Science

Applying GIS, spatial analysis, machine learning, and econometric methods to understand urban and regional systems.

## Transport and Mobility

Investigating how transport infrastructure influences accessibility, mobility behaviour, environmental quality, and regional development.

## Urban and Regional Planning

Examining how infrastructure investment and urban development shape spatial inequalities and regional transformation.

## Environmental Sustainability and Climate Change

Assessing carbon emissions, environmental impacts, and pathways towards sustainable urban and regional development.
